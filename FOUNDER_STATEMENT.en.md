## 🤝 Community Commitments

Despite retaining supreme authority, I commit to the community:

### 1. Power Constraints

- ⚠️ **Major Decision Publicity** - Major decisions like license changes will be publicized 30 days in advance
- ⚠️ **Community Supervision** - Accept community supervision of decisions
- ⚠️ **Transparency** - Financial-related decisions are public
- ⚠️ **Justification** - Provide written justification when vetoing community decisions

### 2. Community Autonomy

- ✅ **Non-Intervention in Daily Operations** - Daily PRs and decisions are made by maintainers
- ✅ **Team Development** - Actively cultivate maintainer team
- ✅ **Power Delegation** - Gradually delegate power to the community
- ✅ **Succession Plan** - Develop and publicize succession plan

### 3. Community Rights Protection

- ✅ **Contributor Recognition** - All contributors receive due recognition
- ✅ **Fair Treatment** - Fair treatment of every community member
- ✅ **Open Communication** - Maintain open communication channels
- ✅ **Shared Benefits** - Project results are shared by the community

## 📋 Principles for Exercising Power

### Normal Usage Scenarios

I commit to exercising founder rights in the following scenarios:

1. **Project Direction Deviation** - When the project deviates from core vision
2. **Community Disputes** - When the community cannot reach consensus
3. **Emergency Situations** - When quick decisions are needed
4. **Quality Issues** - When code quality seriously declines
5. **Security Risks** - When major security vulnerabilities are discovered
6. **Legal Issues** - When legal compliance issues are involved

### Non-Intervention Scenarios

I commit to not intervening in the following scenarios:

1. **Daily PRs** - PRs that maintainers can decide on their own
2. **Technical Details** - Discussions on pure technical implementation
3. **Documentation Improvements** - Documentation and comment improvements
4. **Bug Fixes** - Routine bug fixes
5. **Test Additions** - Addition of test cases

## 🔄 Succession Plan

### Succession Conditions

The succession process will be initiated if I encounter the following situations:

- Unable to continue maintaining the project (health reasons, etc.)
- Voluntarily relinquish founder status
- Long-term non-participation (more than 6 months)
- Other force majeure

### Succession Process

```
1. Founder designates successor candidate
   ↓
2. Community vote (2/3 approval)
   ↓
3. Power transition
   ↓
4. Community announcement
```

### Successor Requirements

- Deep understanding of project vision
- Sufficient time and ability
- Community trust
- Agreement with community co-maintenance philosophy
